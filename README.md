# Uber-Analysis-
Uber Sales &amp; Operations Analysis – Power BI  This project presents a comprehensive Power BI dashboard built to analyze Uber’s operational and sales performance across bookings, revenue, rider patterns, and vehicle utilization. It aims to deliver actionable insights for business decision-making using interactive visualizations and DAX-based metrics.

## key features :

1. Performance KPIs

* Completed Bookings

* Lost Bookings

* Revenue

* Total Distance

* Average Distance

  2. Trend Analysis

* Monthly Bookings & Revenue

* Quarterly Bookings & Revenue

* Monthly Total Distance.

  3. Vehicle Analytics

* Booking Count

* Revenue by Vehicle

* Contribution %

* Total Distance by Vehicle

4. Location Insights

* Top Pickup & Drop Locations

* Busy Time Slots

* Busy Areas

5. Rider Analysis

* Cancellation Reasons

* Payment Method Patterns

* First Riders, Return Riders, Regular Riders

* Monthly & Quarterly Rider Trends.

6. Revenue Insights

* Revenue by Customer

* Revenue by Vehicle

* Revenue by Payment Method


## Outcome 

The dashboard provides a clear view of Uber’s operational performance, helping identify:

High-performing vehicles

Busy operating zones and time slots

Best revenue contributors






Key cancellation patterns

Rider behavior trends

These insights can support strategic decisions in operations, fleet planning, customer engagement, and revenue optimization.
